Welcome to Task Board

A collaborative task management system designed to help teams organize, track, and complete tasks efficiently.

A board is the core organizational unit in Task Board. Think of it as a dedicated workspace for a project, team, or area of responsibility:

Task Board provides a simple yet powerful interface to manage tasks across different projects. Whether you are working solo or with a team, our platform helps you stay organized and focused on what matters most.

Key Features

Board Management

Create and manage multiple boards for different projects or teams. Keep your tasks organized by context.

Collaboration

Invite team members to your boards and assign them tasks. Work together seamlessly on shared projects.

Task Prioritization

Set priorities for tasks (low, medium, high) to ensure the most important work gets done first.

Due Dates

Set deadlines for tasks and get visual indicators when due dates are approaching.

Task Comments

Discuss tasks right where the work happens with our built-in comment system.

Advanced Filtering

Filter tasks by status, assignee, priority, and more to find what you are looking for quickly.

Getting Started

1. Create an account

Sign up with your email address to create a new account or login if you already have one.

2. Create your first board

From the dashboard, click on New Board and give it a name and description.

3. Add tasks

Click on Add Task to create your first task. Assign it to yourself or team members, set priorities, and due dates.

4. Invite collaborators

Share your board with team members by entering their email addresses in the Invite section.

5. Track progress

As tasks are completed, mark them as done to keep track of your progress and see what is left to do.
